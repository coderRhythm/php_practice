Welcome to Disaster Management System

Our mission is to provide comprehensive disaster management solutions to help communities prepare, respond, and recover from various disasters.

Disaster attacks can happen unexpectedly. It's crucial to be prepared and take necessary precautions to minimize the impact. Here are some useful precautions:

  • Have an emergency kit ready with essential supplies such as water, food, medications, and first aid supplies.
  • Create a family communication plan to stay connected during emergencies.
  • Stay informed about potential disasters and evacuation routes in your area.
  • Secure your home by reinforcing doors and windows, and securing heavy furniture.

About Us

Welcome to the Disaster Management Agency (DMA). We are a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting disaster preparedness, response, and recovery efforts in the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure the safety and resilience of our community by providing effective disaster management solutions, education, and support services.

Our Vision

We envision a world where communities are empowered to mitigate the impact of disasters and thrive in the face of adversity.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions.
  • Compassion: We demonstrate empathy and compassion towards those affected by disasters.
  • Collaboration: We foster collaboration and partnerships with stakeholders to enhance disaster resilience.
  • Empowerment: We empower individuals and communities through education and training to prepare for and respond to disasters.
  • Resilience: We promote resilience-building efforts to help communities recover and rebuild stronger after disasters.

Our History

Established in 2005, the Disaster Management Agency has been serving the community for over 15 years. Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to improve disaster preparedness and response capabilities, conduct outreach and education programs, and provide assistance to those affected by disasters.

For more information about our organization and how you can get involved, please contact us.

Earthquake

Earthquakes can cause widespread devastation. Follow these precautions to stay safe:

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On during an earthquake.
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects that can fall.
  • Have an earthquake emergency kit ready.

Flooding

Flooding can occur due to heavy rainfall or storms. Here's what you can do:

  • Stay away from flooded areas and moving water.
  • Evacuate if instructed by authorities.
  • Move to higher ground if flooding is imminent.

Wildfire

Wildfires spread quickly and can be dangerous. Take these precautions:

  • Follow evacuation orders if issued.
  • Clear debris and flammable materials from around your home.
  • Have a fire extinguisher and emergency supplies ready.
